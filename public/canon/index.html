Contemporary Ukrainian Art Painting

CANON

oil on linen canvas, framed, 80cm x 60cm

Presenting an exquisite art painting by the renowned Ukrainian artist Yuri Polchenko (Yu Polch), crafted in 2003 as a heartfelt gift for his sister. This captivating artwork showcases a distinctive style and content, making it a valuable addition to your home gallery or art collection.

Drawing inspiration from the techniques employed by the old masters, the artist skillfully employed traditional painting methods, resulting in a delightful craquelure effect on the surface. Furthermore, by intentionally distressing the linen canvas, the painting acquired a mesmerizing aura reminiscent of a treasured antique masterpiece.

    Interestingly, I originally gifted this particular painting to my sister. However, due to various circumstances, she has decided to sell it. I see that as the rare opportunity for someone who can acquire this wonderful oil painting on linen canvas, which possesses an intriguing metaphorical and poetic narrative.
